The best movies according to Usman

My top 3 movies of all time!

The Great Gatsby

I really enjoyed the way the story was told, the artistry and the acting.

Fast and Furious: Tokyo Drift

This holds a nostalgic placing as it was the first movie I went to watch in the cinema with my friends. I loved the RX7 that Han drove and the drifting through Tokyo was very cool to see as a young teen.

The Gentlemen

The acting, the story, the twists, the turns. What a movie! You won't be disappointed.
